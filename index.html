Defender

The Defender is the ultimate barrier against hacker attacks. You are responsible for protecting the city's vital systems by setting up firewalls, detecting intrusions, and recovering sensitive data before it falls into the wrong hands. Your mission is to thwart the hackers' attempts and ensure the safety of your allies.

Special Skill: Setting up advanced security systems, intrusion monitoring.

Hacker

As a Hacker, you are the brilliant mind who manipulates systems, bypasses security, and infiltrates networks. Your goal is to sow chaos by using your hacking skills to breach defenses and access sensitive information. Use your abilities strategically to deceive your opponents and take control of the city.

Special Skill: Remote hacking, exploiting security vulnerabilities.
